Our Mission

CyberCrimeFighter Books was created with one goal: to make professional cybersecurity education accessible, practical, and affordable for everyone.

In a field that's often dominated by expensive certifications and theoretical knowledge, we focus on hands-on skills that you can apply immediately.

About Christopher Bell (Kdairatchi)

With over a decade of experience in cybersecurity, Christopher has worked as a penetration tester, security consultant, and security educator.

Known online as Kdairatchi, he's passionate about demystifying cybersecurity concepts and helping newcomers enter the field.

His approach focuses on practical, hands-on learning with real-world applications.
